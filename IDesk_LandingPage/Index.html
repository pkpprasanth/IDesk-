Building World's Largest Interviewer Community

Interviewdesk is world’s leading On Demand Interviewer Platform built to disrupt traditional hiring methods and works on a unique concept of outsourced interviewing. Founded in the year 2017, by an ex Amazonian, Interviewdesk is headquartered in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India. With our curated approach, organizations can now save up to 50% of time and cost spent on hiring. In short, we are primarily here to create exceptional interviewing experience for the job seekers, employers as well as the interviewers.

Our Services

Interviews Outsourcing

We specialize in carrying out interviews as a service. To make this possible, our team of hand-picked interviewing experts help organizations find the right candidate at the right time.

Pre-assessed Candidates

Finding the best suited candidate is no more a fairytale. We offer pre-assessed candidates, wherein organizations can leverage on ready to be hired bunch of candidates anytime.

Talent Sourcing

Consider the countless resumes, companies have to cast their eyes over in order to pick the choicest of them. We have charted out a mechanism wherein our team of experts help you with sourcing the most pertinent resumes.
